# Analysis of the Disney Adventure Cruise Article

## Abstract

This document provides a grammatical analysis, including a glossary and notes on idioms, of a Spanish article about Disney's new cruise ship, the Disney Adventure.

## Raw Text (Spanish)

Disney planea lanzar su crucero más grande, el Disney Adventure. El barco tiene capacidad para 6700 pasajeros y comenzará a realizar viajes cortos desde Singapur. Cada viaje durará tres o cuatro noches. Esta es la primera vez que Disney enviará un crucero a esta parte de Asia. La compañía quiere expandirse fuera de Estados Unidos.

Cada vez son menos los turistas internacionales que eligen Estados Unidos como destino de viaje. Debido a este cambio, Disney busca nuevas oportunidades en Asia. La empresa ya cuenta con parques temáticos en varias ciudades asiáticas, pero no tiene ningún crucero en el sudeste asiático. Sus directivos consideran que esta región es un mercado importante con un fuerte crecimiento futuro.

El barco ofrece muchas atracciones basadas en famosas historias de Disney, como Marvel y Frozen. Familias de diferentes edades pueden viajar juntas y disfrutar de las actividades. Disney también tiene planes de expansión y quiere añadir cinco barcos más entre 2027 y 2031.

## English Translation

Disney plans to launch its largest cruise ship, the Disney Adventure. The ship has a capacity for 6700 passengers and will begin to make short trips from Singapore. Each trip will last three or four nights. This is the first time that Disney will send a cruise ship to this part of Asia. The company wants to expand outside the United States.

Fewer and fewer international tourists are choosing the United States as a travel destination. Due to this change, Disney is looking for new opportunities in Asia. The company already has theme parks in several Asian cities, but it does not have any cruise ships in Southeast Asia. Its executives consider that this region is an important market with strong future growth.

The ship offers many attractions based on famous Disney stories, such as Marvel and Frozen. Families of different ages can travel together and enjoy the activities. Disney also has expansion plans and wants to add five more ships between 2027 and 2031.

## Grammatical Analysis

### Verb Tenses

The text uses a mix of present and future tenses to describe Disney's plans.

*   **Present Tense (Indicativo)**: Used to state facts and current plans.
    *   `planea` (plans), `tiene` (has), `es` (is), `quiere` (wants), `son` (are), `eligen` (choose), `busca` (looks for), `cuenta con` (has), `consideran` (consider), `ofrece` (offers), `pueden` (can).
*   **Future Tense (Futuro Simple)**: Used for future actions that are certain or planned.
    *   `comenzará` (will begin), `durará` (will last), `enviará` (will send).

### Noun-Adjective Agreement

Adjectives in Spanish must agree in gender and number with the nouns they modify.

*   `crucero más grande` (largest cruise ship - masculine, singular)
*   `viajes cortos` (short trips - masculine, plural)
*   `turistas internacionales` (international tourists - masculine, plural)
*   `nuevas oportunidades` (new opportunities - feminine, plural)
*   `ciudades asiáticas` (Asian cities - feminine, plural)
*   `mercado importante` (important market - masculine, singular)
*   `fuerte crecimiento` (strong growth - masculine, singular)
*   `famosas historias` (famous stories - feminine, plural)

### Other Grammatical Structures

*   **`deber` + de + infinitive**: The phrase `Debido a este cambio` means "Due to this change".
*   **`contar con`**: This verbal phrase means "to have" or "to be equipped with".
*   **`basadas en`**: The past participle `basadas` is used as an adjective and agrees with `atracciones` (feminine, plural).

## Idioms and Expressions

*   **`Cada vez son menos`**: This is a common idiomatic expression that means "fewer and fewer" or "decreasingly". It is used to describe a trend over time.
