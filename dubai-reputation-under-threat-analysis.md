# Analysis of Dubai's Reputation Under Threat

## 1. Original Spanish Text

Dubái lleva años forjándose una reputación como refugio para turistas, inversores y trabajadores internacionales.

Sin embargo, los recientes ataques con drones y misiles han empezado a poner en peligro esta imagen. Aunque la ciudad ha logrado defenderse, ahora mucha gente está preocupada por el futuro.

Por primera vez, el milagro del éxito y la seguridad de Dubái se ve puesto a prueba por la realidad. Mientras que algunos expatriados se sienten seguros y deciden quedarse, muchos otros han opts por volver a sus países de origen. Dubái atrae a la gente con impuestos bajos y una alta calidad de vida, pero este estilo de vida depende de la paz en la región. Los expertos señalan que la economía de la ciudad está estrechamente ligada al transporte de petróleo y gas a través del cercano estrecho de Ormuz. Si esta zona sigue siendo peligrosa, podría perjudicar la posición de Dubái como centro global.

La ciudad también es criticada por el trato que da a los trabajadores con salarios bajos que construyeron su famoso horizonte. Mientras que algunos dicen que las condiciones están mejorando, otros creen que el sistema explota a los trabajadores extranjeros que nunca podrán obtener la ciudadanía. La pregunta principal ahora es si Dubái puede seguir siendo un lugar al que gente de todo el mundo acude para hacer realidad sus sueños.

## 2. English Translation

Dubai has spent years building a reputation as a refuge for tourists, investors, and international workers.

However, recent drone and missile attacks have begun to endanger this image. Although the city has managed to defend itself, now many people are worried about the future.

For the first time, the miracle of Dubai's success and security is being put to the test by reality. While some expatriates feel safe and decide to stay, many others have chosen to return to their home countries. Dubai attracts people with low taxes and a high quality of life, but this lifestyle depends on peace in the region. Experts point out that the city's economy is closely linked to the transport of oil and gas through the nearby Strait of Hormuz. If this area remains dangerous, it could harm Dubai's position as a global hub.

The city is also criticized for its treatment of low-wage workers who built its famous skyline. While some say conditions are improving, others believe the system exploits foreign workers who can never obtain citizenship. The main question now is whether Dubai can continue being a place where people from around the world come to make their dreams come true.

## 3. Glossary

- **Dubái lleva años forjándose una reputación** — Dubai has spent years building a reputation
  - *llevar + gerundio* = to spend (time) doing something
  - *forjándose* = being forged/built; from *forjarse* (reflexive)
  - *reputación* (f.) = reputation
  - *refugio* (m.) = refuge, shelter
- **para turistas, inversores y trabajadores internacionales** — for tourists, investors, and international workers
  - *turista* (m./f.) = tourist; pl. *turistas*
  - *inversor* (m.) = investor; feminine: *inversora*
  - *trabajador* (m.) = worker; feminine: *trabajadora*
  - *internacional* (m./f. adj.) = international; pl. *internacionales*
- **Sin embargo** — however, nevertheless
- **los recientes ataques con drones y misiles** — recent drone and missile attacks
  - *reciente* (m./f. adj.) = recent; pl. *recientes*
  - *ataque* (m.) = attack; pl. *ataques*
  - *dron* (m.) = drone; pl. *drones*
  - *misil* (m.) = missile; pl. *misiles*
- **han empezado a poner en peligro** — have begun to endanger
  - *empezar* → *han empezado* (present perfect)
  - *poner en peligro* = to endanger (literally "put in danger")
- **esta imagen** — this image
  - *imagen* (f.) = image
- **Aunque la ciudad ha logrado defenderse** — although the city has managed to defend itself
  - *lograr* = to manage to, to achieve
  - *defenderse* = to defend oneself
- **mucha gente está preocupada por el futuro** — many people are worried about the future
  - *preocupada* (f. adj.) = worried; from *preocupar*; masculine: *preocupado*
- **Por primera vez** — for the first time
- **el milagro del éxito y la seguridad** — the miracle of success and security
  - *milagro* (m.) = miracle
  - *éxito* (m.) = success
  - *seguridad* (f.) = security, safety
- **se ve puesto a prueba** — is being put to the test
  - *verse* = to find oneself (reflexive of *ver*)
  - *puesto a prueba* = put to the test; from *poner a prueba*
- **por la realidad** — by reality
- **Mientras que algunos expatriados se sienten seguros** — while some expatriates feel safe
  - *mientras que* = while, whereas (contrast conjunction)
  - *expatriados* (m. pl.) = expatriates; singular: *expatriado*; feminine: *expatriada*
  - *sentirse* → *se sienten* (present, 3rd person pl.)
- **y deciden quedarse** — and decide to stay
  - *decidir* → *deciden* (present, 3rd person pl.)
  - *quedarse* = to stay, to remain
- **muchos otros han opts por volver** — many others have chosen to return
  - *opts* — (misspelling of *optado* / *optado* or the infinitive *optar*)
  - Note: The original text contains "opts" which appears to be a typo/incomplete word. Based on context, it likely should be *han opto* (they have chosen, from *optar*) or *han opts por volver* should be *han opto por volver* — "they have chosen to return"
  - *volver a sus países de origen* = to return to their home countries
  - *país de origen* (m.) = country of origin
- **Dubái atrae a la gente con impuestos bajos** — Dubai attracts people with low taxes
  - *atraer* → *atrae* (present, 3rd person sing.)
  - *impuestos bajos* (m. pl.) = low taxes; singular: *impuesto bajo*
- **y una alta calidad de vida** — and a high quality of life
  - *calidad* (f.) = quality
  - *vida* (f.) = life; pl. *vidas*
- **pero este estilo de vida depende de la paz** — but this lifestyle depends on peace
  - *estilo de vida* (m.) = lifestyle
  - *depender de* = to depend on
  - *paz* (f.) = peace
- **Los expertos señalan que** — experts point out that
  - *señalar* → *señalan* (present, 3rd person pl.)
- **la economía de la ciudad está estrechamente ligada** — the city's economy is closely linked
  - *economía* (f.) = economy
  - *estrechamente* = closely; from *estrecho* (narrow/close) + *-mente*
  - *ligada* (f. adj.) = linked, tied; from *ligar*; masculine: *ligado*
- **al transporte de petróleo y gas** — to the transport of oil and gas
  - *transporte* (m.) = transport
  - *petróleo* (m.) = oil, petroleum
  - *gas* (m., invariable in singular) = gas
- **a través del cercano estrecho de Ormuz** — through the nearby Strait of Hormuz
  - *a través de* = through, across
  - *cercano* (m. adj.) = nearby; feminine: *cercana*
  - *estrecho* (m.) = strait, narrow passage
- **Si esta zona sigue siendo peligrosa** — if this area remains dangerous
  - *zona* (f.) = zone, area
  - *seguir siendo* = to continue being
  - *peligrosa* (f. adj.) = dangerous; masculine: *peligroso*
- **podría perjudicar la posición** — it could harm the position
  - *podría* = could (conditional of *poder*)
  - *perjudicar* = to harm, to damage
  - *posición* (f.) = position
- **como centro global** — as a global hub
  - *global* (m./f. adj.) = global; pl. *globales*
- **La ciudad también es criticada por el trato** — the city is also criticized for the treatment
  - *criticar* → *es criticada* (passive voice)
  - *trato* (m.) = treatment, deal
- **que da a los trabajadores con salarios bajos** — that it gives to low-wage workers
  - *salario bajo* (m.) = low wage; pl. *salarios bajos*
  - *trabajador* (m.) = worker; feminine: *trabajadora*
- **que construyeron su famoso horizonte** — who built its famous skyline
  - *construir* → *construyeron* (preterite, 3rd person pl.)
  - *horizonte* (m.) = horizon, skyline
- **Mientras que algunos dicen que las condiciones están mejorando** — while some say conditions are improving
  - *condición* (f.) = condition; pl. *condiciones*
  - *mejorar* → *están mejorando* (present progressive)
- **otros creen que el sistema explota a los trabajadores extranjeros** — others believe the system exploits foreign workers
  - *sistema* (m.) = system
  - *explotar* = to exploit
  - *extranjero* (m. adj.) = foreign; feminine: *extranjera*
- **que nunca podrán obtener la ciudadanía** — who can never obtain citizenship
  - *obtener* → *podrán obtener* (future: will be able to obtain)
  - *ciudadanía* (f.) = citizenship
- **La pregunta principal ahora es si Dubái puede seguir siendo** — the main question now is whether Dubai can continue being
  - *pregunta* (f.) = question
  - *principal* (m./f. adj.) = main; pl. *principales*
  - *si* = whether (introduces indirect question)
- **un lugar al que gente de todo el mundo acude** — a place where people from all over the world come
  - *lugar* (m.) = place
  - *al que* = to which, where (contracted: *a + el que*)
  - *todo el mundo* = everyone, all the world
  - *acude* = comes to, goes to; from *acudir* (present, 3rd person sing.)
- **para hacer realidad sus sueños** — to make their dreams come true
  - *hacer realidad* = to make come true (literal: "to make reality")
  - *sueño* (m.) = dream; pl. *sueños*

## 4. Grammar Analysis

### "Llevar + Gerundio" — Duration Action
- *Lleva años forjándose* = has spent years building (ongoing action with time marker)
- Similar: *llevo meses estudiando* — I've been studying for months

### Passive "Se" Construction
- *Es criticada* — is criticized (se + 3rd person passive)
- *Se ve puesto a prueba* — is being put to the test (reflexive passive)

### Reflexive for State Changes
- *Forjándose* — building (forging) itself — reflexive for self-action
- *Decide quedarse* — decides to stay (reflexive infinitive)
- *Verse puesto a prueba* — to find oneself being tested

### Por vs. Para — Cause vs. Purpose
- **Por** (*por la realidad*, *por el futuro*): cause, reason
- **Para** (*para tourists*, *para hacer realidad*): purpose, destination

### Subjunctive After Uncertainty
- *Si Dubái puede seguir...* — "si" introduces indirect question, not subjunctive (factual doubt would use subjunctive)
- *Si esta zona sigue siendo peligrosa...* — "si" with future ("remains dangerous") doesn't trigger subjunctive (unreal condition = conditional)

### "Seguir + Gerundio" — Continuing Action
- *Sigue siendo peligrosa* = continues being dangerous
- *Las condiciones están mejorando* = conditions are improving (progressive)

### Relative Pronouns
- *al que* — to which/where (in this context "a place to which people come")
- *donde* would also work but *al que* emphasizes direction toward

### Comparative Structures
- *una alta calidad* — high quality (comparative/superlative marker with *alta*)

### "Empezar a" + Infinitive — Begin to
- *Han empezado a poner en peligro* = have begun to endanger

### Future in the Past
- *Podrían perjudicar* — they could harm (present conditional)
- *Nunca podrán obtener* — they will never be able to obtain (future)

### Por vs. De in Passive
- *Es criticado por* — is criticized by (agent with *por*)
- The worker mistreatment is marked by *por* showing the agent being mistreated: "el trato que da a los trabajadores" — the treatment it gives TO workers
