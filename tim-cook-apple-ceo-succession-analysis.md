# Analysis of Tim Cook's Departure from Apple

## 1. Original Spanish Text

Tim Cook deja el cargo de director ejecutivo de Apple tras quince años al frente de la empresa. Bajo su liderazgo, la empresa se convirtió en un gigante valorado en cuatro billones de dólares y alcanzó un enorme éxito financiero.

Aunque tomó el relevo de un fundador carismático, Cook logró hacer crecer el negocio de forma significativa. Seguirá como presidente para gestionar las relaciones importantes con China y la Casa Blanca, ya que tiene una habilidad única en este ámbito. John Ternus será el nuevo líder de la empresa a partir de este año. Ternus trabajó anteriormente como jefe de ingeniería de hardware y tiene muchas patentes por sus diseños. Los expertos creen que podría ser un líder más práctico en lo que respecta a los nuevos productos en comparación con Cook.

Este cambio llega en un momento en el que mucha gente se pregunta si Apple podrá crear un nuevo dispositivo tan exitoso como el iPhone. Uno de los principales problemas es que Apple se ha quedado atrás en la carrera por la IA. Otras empresas avanzan más rápido, y ahora la compañía debe ponerse al día para seguir siendo líder. El nuevo jefe también deberá lidar con la complicada política global mientras intenta lanzar tecnología revolucionaria.

## 2. English Translation

Tim Cook is leaving the position of CEO of Apple after fifteen years at the helm of the company. Under his leadership, the company became a giant valued at four trillion dollars and achieved enormous financial success.

Although he took over from a charismatic founder, Cook managed to grow the business significantly. He will continue as chairman to manage important relations with China and the White House, since he has a unique skill in this area. John Ternus will be the new leader of the company starting this year. Ternus previously worked as head of hardware engineering and has many patents for his designs. Experts believe he could be a more practical leader regarding new products compared to Cook.

This change comes at a time when many people are wondering if Apple will be able to create a new device as successful as the iPhone. One of the main problems is that Apple has fallen behind in the race for AI. Other companies are advancing faster, and now the company must catch up to remain a leader. The new boss will also have to deal with complicated global politics while trying to launch revolutionary technology.

## 3. Glossary

- **Tim Cook deja el cargo** — Tim Cook leaves the position
  - *dejar* = to leave (to give up, to let go)
  - *cargo* (m.) = position, post, office
- **director ejecutivo** — chief executive officer / CEO (m. noun phrase)
  - *director* (m.) = director, manager
  - *ejecutivo* (m. adj.) = executive
- **de Apple** — of Apple (no article needed with brand names)
- **tras quince años** — after fifteen years
  - *tras* = after (preposition, more formal than *después de*)
  - *quince* = fifteen (cardinal number)
  - *años* (m. pl.) = years; singular: *año*
- **al frente de la empresa** — at the helm of the company
  - *al frente de* = in charge of, leading
  - *empresa* (f.) = company, enterprise
- **bajo su liderazgo** — under his leadership
  - *bajo* = under (preposition)
  - *liderazgo* (m.) = leadership
- **se convirtió en** — became (reflexive: *convertirse en*)
  - *convertir* → *se convirtió* (preterite, 3rd person sing.)
- **un gigante valorado en cuatro billones de dólares** — a giant valued at four trillion dollars
  - *gigante* (m./f. adj. or noun) = giant
  - *valorado en* = valued at
  - *billones* = trillions (in Spanish, *billón* = 10¹², not 10⁹)
  - *dólares* (m. pl.) = dollars
- **alcanzó un enorme éxito financiero** — achieved enormous financial success
  - *alcanzar* → *alcanzó* (preterite)
  - *éxito* (m.) = success
  - *financiero* (m. adj.) = financial; *financiera* (f.)
- **aunque tomó el relevo** — although he took over / although he succeeded
  - *aunque* = although, even though
  - *relevo* (m.) = relay, takeover, succession
  - *tomar el relevo* = to take over (from someone)
- **de un fundador carismático** — from a charismatic founder
  - *fundador* (m.) = founder; feminine: *fundadora*
  - *carismático* (m. adj.) = charismatic; *carismática* (f.)
- **Cook logró hacer crecer el negocio** — Cook managed to grow the business
  - *lograr* = to achieve, to manage to
  - *hacer crecer* = to make grow, to grow (causative)
  - *negocio* (m.) = business
- **de forma significativa** — significantly / in a significant way
  - *forma* (f.) = form, way
  - *significativa* (f. adj.) = significant; *significativo* (m.)
- **seguirá como presidente** — will continue as chairman
  - *seguir* → *seguirá* (future, 3rd person sing.)
  - *presidente* (m.) = president, chairman; feminine: *presidenta*
- **para gestionar las relaciones importantes** — to manage important relations
  - *gestionar* = to manage, to handle (a task/process)
  - *relaciones* (f. pl.) = relations, relationships
- **con China y la Casa Blanca** — with China and the White House
  - *Casa Blanca* (f.) = White House
- **ya que tiene una habilidad única en este ámbito** — since he has a unique skill in this area
  - *ya que* = since, because (conjunction)
  - *habilidad* (f.) = skill, ability
  - *única* (f. adj.) = unique; *único* (m.)
  - *ámbito* (m.) = area, field, scope
- **John Ternus será el nuevo líder** — John Ternus will be the new leader
  - *será* — future of *ser* (will be)
- **a partir de este año** — starting from this year
  - *a partir de* = starting from
- **trabajó anteriormente como jefe de ingeniería de hardware** — previously worked as head of hardware engineering
  - *trabajar* → *trabajó* (preterite)
  - *anteriormente* = previously, before
  - *jefe* (m.) = chief, head; feminine: *jefa*
  - *ingeniería* (f.) = engineering
  - *hardware* (m., invariable) = hardware (English loanword)
- **tiene muchas patentes por sus diseños** — has many patents for his designs
  - *patente* (f.) = patent; plural: *patentes*
  - *diseño* (m.) = design; plural: *diseños*
- **los expertos creen que podría ser** — experts believe he could be
  - *experto* (m.) = expert; feminine: *experta*
  - *creer* → *creen* (present, 3rd person pl.)
  - *podría* — conditional of *poder* (could)
- **un líder más práctico** — a more practical leader
  - *práctico* (m. adj.) = practical; *práctica* (f.)
- **en lo que respecta a los nuevos productos** — regarding new products
  - *respecto a* = regarding, concerning
  - *producto* (m.) = product; plural: *productos*
- **en comparación con Cook** — compared to Cook
  - *comparación* (f.) = comparison
- **este cambio llega en un momento** — this change comes at a time
  - *cambio* (m.) = change
  - *llegar* = to arrive, to come
- **en el que mucha gente se pregunta** — in which many people wonder/ask themselves
  - *preguntarse* = to wonder, to ask oneself
- **si Apple podrá crear** — if Apple will be able to create
  - *podrá* — future of *poder* (will be able)
- **un nuevo dispositivo tan exitoso como el iPhone** — a new device as successful as the iPhone
  - *dispositivo* (m.) = device
  - *tan...como* = as...as
  - *iPhone* (proper noun, no article)
- **uno de los principales problemas** — one of the main problems
  - *principal* (m./f. adj.) = main, principal; pl. *principales*
- **Apple se ha quedado atrás** — Apple has fallen behind
  - *quedarse* = to become (state change)
  - *atrás* = behind, back (adverb)
  - *quedarse atrás* = to fall behind
- **en la carrera por la IA** — in the race for AI
  - *carrera* (f.) = race, career
  - *IA* (f.) = artificial intelligence (*inteligencia artificial*)
- **otras empresas avanzan más rápido** — other companies advance faster
  - *avanzar* = to advance
  - *más rápido* = faster (comparative)
- **la compañía debe ponerse al día** — the company must catch up
  - *deber* = must, to have to
  - *ponerse al día* = to catch up, to get up to date
- **para seguir siendo líder** — to continue being a leader
  - *seguir siendo* = to continue being
- **el nuevo jefe también deberá lidar** — the new boss will also have to deal
  - *jefe* (m.) = boss, chief
  - *deberá* — future of *deber* (will have to)
  - *lidiar* = to deal with, to cope with (usually difficulties)
- **con la complicada política global** — with complicated global politics
  - *política* (f.) = politics, policy (can be singular or collective)
  - *global* (m./f. adj.) = global; same for both; pl. *globales*
- **mientras intenta lanzar tecnología revolucionaria** — while trying to launch revolutionary technology
  - *mientras* = while (conjunction)
  - *intentar* = to try, to attempt
  - *lanzar* = to launch, to throw
  - *revolucionaria* (f. adj.) = revolutionary; *revolucionario* (m.)

## 4. Grammar Analysis

### Present Perfect with "Se" — Passive Construction
*Apple se ha quedado atrás* — Apple has fallen behind
- *quedarse* + past participle forms a passive-like construction
- Similar to: *se ha convertido en* (has become), *se ha convertido* (has been converted)

### "Al" + Infinitive — After/Upon
*al frente de la empresa* — at the head of the company
- *al + infinitive* = upon + -ing: *al llegar* (upon arriving), *al frente de* (at/in charge of)

### Por vs. Para — Cause vs. Purpose
- **Por** (*por sus diseños* = for his designs) — cause, reason, exchange
- **Para** (*para gestionar* = to manage) — purpose, destination, recipient

### Subjunctive After Uncertainty
*si Apple podrá crear* uses indicative because it's a yes/no question expecting an answer. If it were *si Apple pudiera crear* (if Apple could...), it would be subjunctive (doubt).

### Subjunctive After "Creer que" vs. "No Creer que"
- *Creen que podría ser* — they believe (indicative, certainty)
- *No creen que sea posible* — they don't believe (subjunctive, doubt)

### Comparatives
- *más práctico en lo que respecta a* — more practical regarding (superlative-like)
- *tan exitoso como* — as successful as (equality comparative)

### Reflexive Verbs for State Changes
- *se convirtió en* — became
- *se ha quedado atrás* — has fallen behind
- *se pregunta* — wonders (asks himself)

### Future Tense for Probability
*Cook seguirá como presidente* — Cook will continue (future tense, not just "shall/will")

### "Deber + Infinitive" — Obligation
- *debe ponerse al día* — must catch up
- *deberá lidar* — will have to deal

- **en comparación con** — compared to
- **quedarse atrás** — to fall behind, to be left behind
- **la carrera por la IA** — the AI race
- **ponerse al día** — to catch up, to get up to date

- **lanzar tecnología revolucionaria** — to launch revolutionary technology
