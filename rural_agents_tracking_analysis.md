# Analysis of Rural Agents' Tracking Operation

## Raw Text (Spanish)

Según Pallarés, la zona de alto riesgo, una primera corona de seis kilómetros, ya está analizada, por lo que ahora los Agentes Rurales se están centrando en rastrear la segunda, hasta completar un radio de 20 kilómetros.

## English Translation

According to Pallarés, the high-risk zone, a first perimeter of six kilometers, has already been analyzed, so now the Rural Agents are focusing on tracking the second one, until completing a 20-kilometer radius.

## Analysis

The text describes an ongoing operation by Rural Agents, likely related to a search or tracking effort. According to Pallarés, a primary high-risk zone (a six-kilometer perimeter) has been thoroughly analyzed. The current focus of the agents is to track a secondary zone, extending their efforts to cover a 20-kilometer radius. This implies a systematic and expanding search operation, possibly in response to a threat or incident.

## Glossary

*   `Según` - According to
*   `zona de alto riesgo` - high-risk zone
*   `primera corona` - first perimeter/ring
*   `kilómetros` - kilometers
*   `ya está analizada` - has already been analyzed
*   `por lo que` - so, which is why
*   `ahora` - now
*   `Agentes Rurales` - Rural Agents
*   `se están centrando` - they are focusing
*   `rastrear` - to track, to trace
*   `segunda` - second (one)
*   `hasta completar` - until completing
*   `radio` - radius

## Grammar Analysis

*   **Present Tense:** Used to describe current states and ongoing actions.
    *   `está analizada` (has been analyzed / is analyzed) - Present tense of "estar" with a past participle to indicate a state resulting from a past action.
    *   `se están centrando` (they are focusing) - Present progressive to indicate an ongoing action.
*   **Gerunds (-ando):** Used to express continuous action.
    *   `centrando` (focusing) - Part of the present progressive.
*   **Infinitive after Preposition:**
    *   `hasta completar` (until completing) - Infinitive used after "hasta" (until) to express the endpoint of an action.
*   **Prepositional Phrases:**
    *   `Según Pallarés` (According to Pallarés)
    *   `de alto riesgo` (of high risk)
    *   `de seis kilómetros` (of six kilometers)
    *   `en rastrear` (on tracking)
    *   `de 20 kilómetros` (of 20 kilometers)
*   **Passive Voice / State:** `ya está analizada` indicates a state of being analyzed, implicitly passive.
