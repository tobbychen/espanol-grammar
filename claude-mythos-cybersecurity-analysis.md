# Analysis of Claude Mythos AI Cybersecurity Concerns

## 1. Original Spanish Text

Un nuevo modelo de IA llamado Claude Mythos está causando preocupación a nivel mundial por sus capacidades en materia de ciberseguridad.

Desarrollado por la empresa estadounidense Anthropic, el modelo se entrenó en programación, pero llegó a ser excepcionalmente bueno detectando vulnerabilidades que los humanos habían pasado por alto durante décadas.

En las pruebas, incluso logró que un usuario sin ningún tipo de permisos accediera a un administrador militar estadounidense. Lo que más preocupa a los expertos es su capacidad para encadenar pequeñas debilidades y convertirlas en una ruta de ataque sofisticada.

Para evitar que los delincuentes lo utilizan, Anthropic mantiene el modelo muy restringido a través de un proyecto llamado Glass Wing, que solo concede acceso a socios estadounidenses seleccionados. Sin embargo, esto genera tensión geopolítica. Países como la India argumentan que el acceso limitado perjudica su soberanía digital y los deja rezagados. Aunque los líderes mundiales debatieron las normas de seguridad de la IA en una cumbre en febrero de 2026, crear leyes internacionales es difícil porque los gobiernos compiten por controlar esta poderosa tecnología.

## 2. English Translation

A new AI model called Claude Mythos is causing worldwide concern due to its cybersecurity capabilities.

Developed by the American company Anthropic, the model was trained in programming, but came to be exceptionally good at detecting vulnerabilities that humans had overlooked for decades.

In tests, it even managed to allow a user with no permissions at all to access a US military administrator. What worries experts most is its ability to chain small weaknesses and convert them into a sophisticated attack route.

To prevent criminals from using it, Anthropic keeps the model highly restricted through a project called Glass Wing, which only grants access to selected US partners. However, this generates geopolitical tension. Countries like India argue that limited access harms their digital sovereignty and leaves them lagging behind. Although world leaders debated AI security standards at a summit in February 2026, creating international laws is difficult because governments compete to control this powerful technology.

## 4. Grammar Analysis

### Key Patterns

1. **Idiom: *pasar por alto*** — to overlook, to miss. Literally "to pass by on high." Used here in the phrase "vulnerabilidades que los humanos habían pasado por alto" (vulnerabilities that humans had overlooked).

2. **Past Perfect Tense (*habían pasado*):** The phrase "los humanos habían pasado por alto" uses the pluperfect tense to describe an action completed before another past point. Structure: *haber* (imperfect) + past participle.

3. **Infinitive after preposition *en*:** "en materia de ciberseguridad" = in terms of cybersecurity. The phrase *en materia de* means "on the subject of" or "in terms of."

4. **Reflexive construction for emphasis:** "lo que más preocupa a los expertos" = what worries experts most. The pronoun *lo* + *que* introduces a relative clause acting as the subject.

5. **Por vs. Para:** In this article, *por* is used in "por su capacidad" (for its capacity) indicating cause/reason, while *para* would indicate purpose.

6. **Adjective agreement:** *sofisticada* agrees in feminine singular with *ruta de ataque* (route is feminine); *rezagados* is masculine plural matching *países*.

## 5. Idioms and Expressions

- **pasar por alto** — to overlook, to miss, to disregard. Literally "to pass by on high." Expresses failing to notice or ignoring something important.

- **dejar rezagado** — to leave behind, to cause to lag. Used to describe countries or groups being left at a disadvantage. *Los deja rezagados* = it leaves them behind.

- **en materia de** — in terms of, on the subject of. Used to introduce a topic area. *En materia de ciberseguridad* = in terms of cybersecurity.

- **competir por controlar** — to compete to control. Expresses rivalry over gaining control of something. Note that *por* here indicates the goal of competition.
