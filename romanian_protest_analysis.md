# Spanish Text Analysis: Romanian Education Protests

## Raw Spanish Text

```spanish
Miles de profesores y personal educativo rumanos protestaron contra los planes del Gobierno de recortar la financiación de la educación.

El Gobierno, liderado por el primer ministro Ilie Bolojan, aprobó medidas de austeridad para reducir el déficit presupuestario. Entre ellas se incluye un recorte del 10 % en el fondo salarial de las instituciones públicas, lo que, según los profesores, perjudicará a las escuelas y universidades. Los manifestantes argumentaron que la educación ya recibe menos dinero, y los rectores de las universidades advirtieron que estos recortes limitarán el desarrollo y la investigación. Algunos compararon Rumanía con Ucrania, donde el gasto en educación ha aumentado incluso durante la guerra, y cuestionaron por qué Rumanía hace lo contrario. Los sindicatos de profesores han amenazado con ir a la huelga si se aplican las medidas. También han iniciado una petición para recoger 100 000 firmas con el fin de revocar los planes del Gobierno. El nuevo presupuesto con estas medidas se enviará al Parlamento antes del 20 de febrero, y los sindicatos podrían anunciar fechas de huelga para marzo o junio.
```

## English Translation

```english
Thousands of Romanian teachers and educational staff protested against the government's plans to cut education funding.

The government, led by Prime Minister Ilie Bolojan, approved austerity measures to reduce the budget deficit. These include a 10% cut in the salary fund of public institutions, which, according to teachers, will harm schools and universities. Demonstrators argued that education already receives less money, and university rectors warned that these cuts will limit development and research. Some compared Romania with Ukraine, where spending on education has increased even during the war, and questioned why Romania is doing the opposite. Teachers' unions have threatened to go on strike if the measures are applied. They have also initiated a petition to collect 100,000 signatures in order to revoke the government's plans. The new budget with these measures will be sent to Parliament before February 20, and unions could announce strike dates for March or June.
```

*   `Miles de profesores y personal educativo rumanos` (Masculine Plural, adjective `rumanos` agrees)

### Reflexive Verbs and Passive Constructions
*   **se incluye**: "is included" - An impersonal/reflexive passive construction, stating a fact without specifying an agent.
*   **se aplican**: "are applied" - Another impersonal/reflexive passive construction used in the conditional clause.
*   **se enviará**: "will be sent" - An impersonal/reflexive passive construction for a future action.

### Use of Prepositions
Various prepositions are used to show relationships, direction, and purpose:
*   **contra los planes**: "against the plans" (opposition)
*   **de recortar**: "to cut" (complement of `planes`)
*   **de la educación**: "of education" (possession/scope)
*   **liderado por**: "led by" (agency)
*   **para reducir**: "to reduce" (purpose)
*   **en el fondo salarial**: "in the salary fund" (location)
*   **de las instituciones**: "of the institutions" (possession)
*   **a las escuelas y universidades**: "to schools and universities" (indirect object)
*   **con Ucrania**: "with Ukraine" (comparison)
*   **durante la guerra**: "during the war" (duration)
*   **con ir a la huelga**: "to go on strike" (complement of `amenazado`)
*   **con el fin de revocar**: "in order to revoke" (purpose)
*   **al Parlamento**: "to Parliament" (destination)
*   **antes del 20 de febrero**: "before February 20" (time)
*   **para marzo o junio**: "for March or June" (timeframe)

*   **reducir**: to reduce - To make something smaller or less in amount.
*   **déficit presupuestario**: budget deficit - The amount by which spending exceeds income over a particular period.
*   **recorte**: cut (noun) - A reduction in amount or size.

*   **instituciones públicas**: public institutions - Organizations established for public service, funded by the state.
*   **perjudicará**: will harm - (Future of `perjudicar`) Will cause damage or injury to.
*   **escuelas y universidades**: schools and universities - Educational institutions.
*   **manifestantes**: demonstrators, protesters - People who publicly show opposition to something.
*   **argumentaron**: argued - (Preterite of `argumentar`) Presented reasons for or against an idea.

*   **advirtieron**: warned - (Preterite of `advertir`) Gave notice of potential danger or problem.
