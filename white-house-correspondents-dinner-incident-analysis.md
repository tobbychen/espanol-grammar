# Analysis of White House Correspondents' Dinner Incident

## 1. Original Spanish Text

El sábado por la noche se desató el caos en la cena de los corresponsales de la Casa Blanca cuando un hombre armado con varias armas intentó atacar.

El presidente Donald Trump, la primera dama y el vicepresidente JD Vance fueron evacuados rápidamente de la sala por agentes del Servicio Secreto fuertemente armados.

Mientras los invitados y los periodistas se escondían debajo de las mesas, las fuerzas de seguridad detuvieron al sospechoso justo fuera del salón de baile. Un agente recibió un disparo, pero sobrevivió gracias a su chaleco antibalas. El sospechoso ha sido identificado como Cole Thomas Allen, un hombre de 31 años de California. La policía descubrió que llevaba una escopeta, una pistola y varios cuchillos. Se alojaba en el hotel Washington Hilton, donde tuvo lugar el evento, lo que ha suscitado serias dudas sobre cómo pudo acercarse tanto al presidente con tantas armas.

El incidente también ha causado preocupación a la familia real británica. El rey Carlos y la reina Camila tenían previsto visitar Washington el lunes, pero las autoridades están revisando ahora los planes de seguridad para decidir si el viaje es seguro. Esta es la tercera gran amenaza a la seguridad a la que se ha enfrentado el presidente Trump en los últimos dos años.

## 2. English Translation

On Saturday night, chaos erupted at the White House Correspondents' Dinner when an armed man with multiple weapons attempted to attack.

President Donald Trump, the First Lady, and Vice President JD Vance were quickly evacuated from the room by heavily armed Secret Service agents.

While guests and journalists hid under the tables, security forces detained the suspect just outside the ballroom. An agent was shot, but survived thanks to his bulletproof vest. The suspect has been identified as Cole Thomas Allen, a 31-year-old man from California. Police discovered he was carrying a shotgun, a pistol, and several knives. He was staying at the Washington Hilton Hotel, where the event took place, which has raised serious doubts about how he could get so close to the president with so many weapons.

The incident has also caused concern for the British royal family. King Charles and Queen Camilla were planning to visit Washington on Monday, but authorities are now reviewing security plans to decide if the trip is safe. This is the third major security threat that President Trump has faced in the last two years.

## 4. Grammar Analysis

### Passive Voice with "Se" (Pasiva refleja)
*"El caos se desató"* — Uses *se* + third person singular verb to form passive voice. The subject (chaos) receives the action.
- English equivalent: "Chaos was unleashed"

### Preterite vs. Imperfect Tense
- **Preterite (*intentó*, *detuvieron*, *recibió*):** Completed actions in past
- **Imperfect (*se escondían*, *se alojaba*, *tenían previsto*):** Ongoing, repeated, or background actions

### Reflexive Constructions
- *se desató* — passive/reflexive
- *se escondían* — "were hiding themselves"
- *se alojaba* — "was staying" (at a place, reflexive)

### "Lo que" Clause
*"lo que ha suscitado serias dudas"* — "which has raised serious doubts"
- *lo que* = "that which" / "which" (relative pronoun for entire previous clause)

### Present Perfect
*"se ha enfrentado"* — has faced (present perfect 3rd sing.)
- Used to connect past event to present consequences

### Por vs. Para
- *fueron evacuados rápidamente **de** la sala* — movement away from
- *para decidir si el viaje es seguro* — purpose/goal
