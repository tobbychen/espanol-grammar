# Analysis of Mbappé's Struggles at Real Madrid

## 1. Original Spanish Text

Kylian Mbappé está pasando por un mal momento en el Real Madrid. Aunque ha marcado 85 goles en dos temporadas, ha surgido una petición online masiva en la que se pide al club que lo venda.

Según se informa, la petición cuenta con más de 30 millones de firmas, aunque los expertos dicen que muchas de ellas podrían ser de bots, ya que 30 millones es una gran parte de la población española. Los aficionados están descontentos por varias razones. En primer lugar, el Real Madrid está pasando por un mal momento y es probable que no gane ningún título importante esta temporada, mientras que su rival, el Barcelona, está rindiendo muy bien. En segundo lugar, mientras Mbappé estaba lesionado, se le vio disfrutando de unas vacaciones de lujo en Cerdeña con su novia, lo que los aficionados interpretaron como una falta de compromiso con el club. Algunos expertos también creen que tener a Mbappé y a Vinícius Jr. en el mismo equipo desequilibra la alineación. Incluso hay rumores de que, si José Mourinho se convierte en el próximo entrenador, podría no tolerar el estilo de vida y el ego de Mbappé.

## 2. English Translation

Kylian Mbappé is going through a difficult time at Real Madrid. Although he has scored 85 goals in two seasons, a massive online petition has emerged requesting that the club sell him.

As reported, the petition has more than 30 million signatures, although experts say many of them could be from bots, since 30 million is a large portion of the Spanish population. Fans are unhappy for several reasons. First, Real Madrid is going through a bad moment and will likely not win any major title this season, while their rival, Barcelona, is performing very well. Second, while Mbappé was injured, he was seen enjoying luxury vacations in Sardinia with his girlfriend, which fans interpreted as a lack of commitment to the club. Some experts also believe that having Mbappé and Vinícius Jr. on the same team unbalances the lineup. There are even rumors that, if José Mourinho becomes the next coach, he might not tolerate Mbappé's lifestyle and ego.

## 4. Grammar Analysis

**1. Progressive construction "está pasando":**
- *Mbappé está pasando por un mal momento* — Mbappé is going through a difficult time
- *estar + gerund* expresses an ongoing action or state in progress

**2. Subjunctive after expressions of request:**
- *se pide al club que lo venda* — "they ask the club to sell him"
- After expressions of wanting/requesting (*pedir que*), the verb in the subordinate clause is in the subjunctive (*venda*).

**3. Subjunctive after expressions of uncertainty/doubt:**
- *expertos dicen que muchas podrían ser de bots* — "experts say many could be from bots" (this is indicative because it's stated as a possibility with "podrían")
- Compare: *es probable que no gane ningún título* — "it is likely that [Real Madrid] won't win any title" — here the uncertainty triggers subjunctive (*no gane*).

**4. "Según se informa" — passive reflexive:**
- *Según se informa* — "as reported," "according to information"
- This construction uses *se* as an impersonal passive marker: *se informa que...* = "it is reported that..."

**5. "Se le vio disfrutando" — perception with reflexive:**
- *se le vio disfrutando* — "he was seen enjoying"
- The construction *ver + object + gerund* is used for perception: *La vi cantando* (I saw her singing).
- The indirect object *le* refers to Mbappé.

**6. "Desequilibra la alineación" — verb with direct object:**
- *desequilibra* = third person singular of *desequilibrar* (to unbalance)
- *la alineación* = direct object (the lineup)
- No reflexive needed because the action affects the lineup, not the subject performing the action.

**7. Conditional for speculation:**
- *podría no tolerar* — "might not tolerate"
- *Podría* is the conditional of *poder* (can/to be able to), used here for speculation about a hypothetical future situation.

**8. "Si José Mourinho se convierte" — subjunctive in conditional:**
- The "if" clause expressing a future possibility uses the subjunctive (*se convierta*), not the indicative, because it represents an uncertain hypothetical.

**9. "Lo vende" vs "venderlo":**
- *lo venda* (subjunctive after *pedir que*) vs *venderlo* (infinitive after preposition)

**10. Contrast with "mientras que":**
- *mientras que su rival... está rindiendo* — "while their rival... is performing"
- *mientras* introduces a contrast between two simultaneous situations.
